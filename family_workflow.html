DawaiSathi v4.1 — Family Workflow Guide

DawaiSathi v4.1 empowers families and caregivers to manage medicine schedules, track adherence, and receive critical dose reminders together seamlessly.

🔑 2. Family Group Creation

  • Auto-Created Personal Family: Every new user is automatically assigned a dedicated Family group upon registration (e.g. "Ayaan's Family Group") with a unique 6-digit numeric code (e.g. 582914).
  • Custom Family Group Naming: Admins can customize their group name (e.g. "Gupta Family Medicine Hub") directly from the Family Settings tab by tapping the pencil icon.

📲 3. 1-Tap Direct Joining & Sharing Options

DawaiSathi v4.1 provides 3 friction-free ways for relatives to join:

Option A: 🚀 1-Tap WhatsApp Invite Link (Recommended)

  • How it works: The admin taps Share on WhatsApp.
  • Link format: https://dawaisathi-api.onrender.com/home?join_code=582914
  • Result: Relatives tap the link on WhatsApp, and DawaiSathi opens directly to the Join Confirmation modal with the 6-digit code pre-filled!

Option B: 📷 QR Code Camera Scan

  • How it works: Display the QR code in Family Settings.
  • Result: Relatives scan the QR code with their phone camera to instantly trigger the join flow.

Option C: 🔢 6-Digit Manual OTP Entry

  • How it works: Relatives open Family tab -> tap Join with a code -> enter digits (582914).

❤️ 5. Caregiver Nudges & Emergency Features

1. Caregiver Dose Reminder Nudge (POST /api/family/nudge)

Caregivers can tap the "❤️ Remind" button on a family member's card to trigger an instant dose reminder alert.

2. 🚑 Family Emergency Helpline Card

Instant 1-tap emergency helpline dial (tel:108) embedded directly in Family Settings.
